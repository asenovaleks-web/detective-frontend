What we collect

When you use Signum without an account, we collect only the domain names you scan. These are stored anonymously to cache results and improve future scan speed — they are not linked to your IP address, device, or identity.

When you create an account, we collect your email address and store your scan history, watchlist, and subscription plan. We do not collect your name, phone number, or card details — payments are handled entirely by Stripe.

How we use your data

  • To deliver scan results and cache them for faster future lookups
  • To manage your account, subscription, and scan history
  • To send transactional emails (alerts, receipts) via Resend
  • To improve the accuracy of our threat detection

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating Signum.

Third-party services

  • Stripe — payment processing. Governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — authentication and database. Data stored in EU region.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery.
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI analysis. Only domain names are sent; no personal data.

Data retention

Anonymous scan cache entries are retained to serve future users faster. Account data is kept for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

Chrome Extension

The Signum Chrome Extension only processes the domain name of the site you explicitly request a scan for. It does not track your browsing history, collect personal data, or run automatically in the background.

  • activeTab / tabs — used to read the URL of the current tab when you click the extension icon
  • windows — used to open the extension as a detached popup window
  • IP address — used solely for rate limiting to prevent abuse; not stored persistently

The extension does not execute remote code. All logic is contained within the extension package.
