Common questions

Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings and you won't be charged again. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Payments are processed securely — we never see your card details.
What counts as a scan?
Each domain you analyse counts as one scan. Results are cached for 6 hours so re-scanning the same domain within that window doesn't use another scan.
Is there a free trial for Pro?
The Free plan lets you run scans and see the verdict and risk score at no cost. Pro unlocks the full intelligence breakdown, scan history, and alerts.
How does the Team plan work?
One billing account covers up to 5 seats. Team members share a watchlist and scan history. Contact us via the pricing page to get set up.
